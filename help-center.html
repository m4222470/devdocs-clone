Creating an account is easy! Simply click the "Sign Up" button on our homepage, enter your email address and create a password. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

To reset your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to create a new password. The link expires after 24 hours.
